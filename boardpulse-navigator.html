Case Study: BoardPulse Navigator

1. Step-by-Step Focus

Traditional board surveys are often long and tiring. BoardPulse Navigator breaks the process into a structured data ingestion piece. The board starts by mapping the Intensity Scores (out of 100) exactly as they appear in their formal BoardPulse 2.0 Report regarding core Board Functions.

2. Honest Self-Assessment

In Step 2, board members use simple sliders to accurately input their board's qualities (like Culture and Expertise) out of 100, perfectly mirroring the Intensity Scores detailed in their formal BoardPulse Report.

Step 2. Assess Qualities
Expertise (Fundraising)

3. Targeted "Fail on Purpose" Questions

The system identifies the 3 lowest-scoring weaknesses from Step 2. For each weakness, it randomly pulls 2 "What if" scenarios from a curated internal bank of 100 AI-generated questions.

Crucially, the app does not digitally brainstorm. It acts as a silent facilitator, displaying prompts for the board to debate verbally in real life. Once the physical 'storm' settles, the members click to 'vote' on their highest-priority provocations, porting them straight into the formal Action Plan.

Step 3. Question Storm
"What if we strictly enforced a 3-strike rule for meeting absenteeism?"
TARGETING: BANDWIDTH

4. Clear Action Plans

Finally, the most useful questions are turned into concrete tasks. The board assigns an owner and a deadline to each action. The final plan can be easily exported as a PDF or shared via WhatsApp to be included directly in the board meeting minutes.
